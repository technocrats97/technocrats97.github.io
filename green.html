Suitable Places For Harvesting of Green Energy.
Factors Required to Set Up a Hydro & Wind Power Station :

1. Avoid regions under restricted areas , sanctuaries and animal reserves.

2. Large amount of water supply,through a dam

(For Hydro Energy) 3. A high altitude region with good cut off speed and optimal speed of wind energy on the wind turbines.(For Wind Energy)

4. Site proximity to a nearest water body with high speed flow of water

5. Least amount of surrounding obstacles such as settlements or forest regions,to avoid unnecessary flooding

6. cost of transportation from nearest city or village

7. Site accessibility

8. future benefits and site maintenance
